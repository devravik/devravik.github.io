Privacy Policy

WA Direct Chat & Tools
Effective Date: March 2026

WA Direct Chat & Tools (“the app”) is designed to help users interact with WhatsApp more efficiently. Your privacy is important to us, and this app is built with a privacy-first approach.

1. No Account Required

This app does not require any login, signup, or personal account. You can use all features without providing personal information.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • No user accounts
  • No personal information
  • No contact data
  • No background tracking

All data stays on your device.

3. How the App Works

The app provides tools such as:

  • Direct chat using WhatsApp links
  • Text formatting and generation
  • Message templates and history
  • QR code generation

All actions are performed locally on your device. The app uses official WhatsApp deep links (https://wa.me/) to open chats.

4. Clipboard Usage

The app may read clipboard content only when you open the app or perform a paste action.

This is used to:

  • Detect phone numbers
  • Help you quickly start a WhatsApp chat

Clipboard data is:

  • Not stored permanently
  • Not uploaded anywhere
  • Not accessed in the background

5. Data Stored on Device

The app may store limited data locally on your device:

  • App settings (theme, country code)
  • Message templates you create
  • Recent activity (history)

This data is stored only on your device and can be cleared anytime from app settings.

6. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

Storage (optional)

Used only when you choose to save or share QR codes.

No other sensitive permissions are required.

7. What We Do Not Do

We strictly do not:

  • Collect or sell user data
  • Track user activity
  • Access contacts or call logs
  • Send messages automatically
  • Enable bulk or spam messaging
  • Access WhatsApp without user action

All actions are fully controlled by the user.

8. Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics or tracking services.
